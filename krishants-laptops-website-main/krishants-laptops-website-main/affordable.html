Information about affordable laptops



Affordable laptops are great for people looking for a good enough performing laptop that is cheap and can be used for school work. A lot of schools have the BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) policy where the student needs to bring their own laptop to school. Cheap chromebooks are a great option for school work as you really only need these devices for writing in documents, Google Classroom, Gmail, etc. Cheap chromebooks are good enough to run these smoothly without any problem. I will highly recommend to buy HP chromebooks as they perform quite well for their price and look aesthetically pleasing. Chromebook prices range from approximately $300 - $1000.
